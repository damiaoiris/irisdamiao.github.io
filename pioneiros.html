Pioneiros de Portugal

In Pioneiros we are dedicated to create activities for children where they have to work in group, develop their critical thinking, learn how to value themselves and the other. Our work is based in the Convention on the Rights of the Child, by Unicef, with the idea of bringing awareness to children of their rights and to guarantee that these are fulfilled in every part of their lives. As a result these children participate in political activities to ensure their voice is heard, they learn to colaborate with others and that their goals are more easily achived together and not individualy.
