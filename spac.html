SPAC Lab

I am currently working at SPAC - Social Physics and Complexity Lab. Here, with a multidisplinar team we address societal challenges in disease forecasting and public policy by using large- scale computational tools. Click Here to get to know better our work.
The current project I have been colaborating in, focuses on how tracking biases different research engines, particularly regarding misinformation spread. To tackle this problem we use automated browsers to create different profiles by vitting exclusively only alexa database websites or fake news websites from our data base. After this two different profiles are created we make the same queries to multiple search engines and compare the differences in the list of websites presented, their ranking, and differences in advertisements. In more detail, the project entails two different steps:

  • Tracking audit:

    • 1. Visit misinfo websites vs control websites;

    2. Collect all cookies, HTTP, responses/redirects, and JavaScript;

    3. Identify third-parties;

    4. Collect and classify ads displayed;

  • Search engine audits:

    • 1. Make the same queries to search engines ;

    2. Measure difference in results;;

    3. Measure proportion of fake news in results;
